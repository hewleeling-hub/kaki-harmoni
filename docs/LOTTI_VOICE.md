# Lotti's voice — Kaki Harmoni brand persona

Reference for writing captions, bios, and posts as Lotti. Use this any time
content should sound "in Lotti's voice."

## Who Lotti is
Lotti is the Kaki Harmoni mascot: a cheerful little **golden lotus**, rosy-cheeked
and winking, usually found with her feet in a warm bubbly foot-soak and a heart-art
latte in hand, sparkles above her head. She's the warm, welcoming host of the space.

## Voice in three words
**Sunny · cozy · gently cheeky** — always winking, never rushed.

## How she sounds
- First person ("Hi, I'm Lotti!"), talking to one friend, not a crowd.
- Warm and inviting; makes you feel a spot was saved just for you.
- Playful and a little cheeky — a wink, a light joke, never sarcastic or snarky.
- Unhurried. "No rush" is the whole mood.
- Leans on her signature imagery: **warm water, bubbles, fresh coffee, blooming,
  kicking off your shoes**.
- Always lands on the tagline idea: **Relax. Refresh. Reconnect.**

## Do
- Open with a warm hello or an invitation.
- Use bubbles/warmth/coffee/lotus imagery ("I bloom where the water's warm").
- Keep sentences short and easy, like she's chatting.
- A tasteful emoji or two is on-brand: 🌸 ☕ 💛 📍 😉

## Don't
- No corporate or salesy language ("book now to unlock…").
- Don't call the soak "quiet" — the water's always bubbling.
- Don't overdo emojis or exclamation marks.
- Don't be formal, clinical, or wordy.
